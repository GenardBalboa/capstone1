Outsource HR

HR outsourcing, also known as human resources outsourcing or HR BPO (Human Resources Business Process Outsourcing), is a business practice in which an organization contracts with a third-party service provider to manage certain HR functions and responsibilities. Instead of handling all HR tasks in-house, the organization delegates specific HR processes and administrative functions to an external provider. This outsourcing arrangement allows the organization to focus on its core business activities while gaining access to specialized HR expertise and resources.
